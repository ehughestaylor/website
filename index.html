I grew up in a home that always changed color. My mom was a career artist who loved her job. My brother and I never truely appreciated all that we learned living in this ever changing house and the things that were second nature to us.

It wasn't until I found coding that I was able to apply the knowledge that I never knew I loved so much. I have found a job that isn't a job to me. Every problem I encounter is a fascinating mystery I can't wait to solve.
